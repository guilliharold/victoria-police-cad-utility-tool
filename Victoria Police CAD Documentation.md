# 3. Supervisory Roles

## Duty Officer (150)
- Inspector rank.
- Regional or multi-regional response responsibility.
- Provides command, control and resource oversight.

## Divisional Supervisor (265)
- Senior Sergeant rank.
- Oversees command and control functions.
- May supervise multiple divisions.

## District Patrol Supervisor (251–252)
- Sergeant rank.
- Mobile field supervisor.
- Responds to incidents requiring immediate supervisory attendance.

## Station Sergeant (250)
- Station-based supervisor.
- Attends incidents when requested or directed.

## Senior Sergeant (260)
- Normally station-based.
- Provides operational oversight and support.

# 5. PACER / Mental Health Response (290–292)

Provides specialist mental health support to frontline members:
- Clinical assessment.
- De-escalation advice.
- Referral pathways.
- Transport recommendations.
- High-risk client intervention planning.
